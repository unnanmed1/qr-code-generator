How It Works

Follow these simple steps to generate and download your QR code:

Step 1

Select the type of QR code you want to generate (e.g., Text, URL, Email, SMS, WIFI, Contact, PayPal, or Bitcoin).

Step 2

Enter the required information in the input fields. For example, enter a URL for a URL QR code or an email address for an Email QR code.

Step 3

Click the "Generate QR Code" button. Your QR code will appear below the form. You can then download it as a PNG image.
